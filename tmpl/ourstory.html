Our Story

Welcome, my name is Brian Hooper and I am the starter for TaleSlide!

The original idea for TaleSlide began in February 2012. As part of a leadership development program within my company. I had the opportunity to work with an amazing team and the core of our team's research was focused on improving our ability to get ideas to spread.

After more than 100 days of intense research, design, and development we presented the first prototype, although we may not have known it at the time. By May of 2012 it was basically just a very basic Single Page HTML5 Web Application that we had quickly developed so that we could present our research. The concept for our presentation was to deliver a front row experience. We wanted our audience to experience how the remarkable blending of story, imagery, mesasging, style, and technology worked together.
